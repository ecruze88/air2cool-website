Many older homes in Morris County were retrofitted for central AC. That means ductwork is often undersized, leaky, or routed through hot attics. The result is weak airflow and uneven cooling.

## Signs of duct airflow problems

- One floor is much warmer than another
- Rooms far from the air handler stay hot
- Whistling or rattling from vents
- Dust buildup around supply registers

## What a professional can check

- Static pressure and total airflow
- Duct leakage in attics or crawlspaces
- Supply and return balance

If the ducts are the problem, fixing them can lower bills and improve comfort more than replacing the AC.
