If your AC is breaking down, the big question is whether to repair it or replace it. In Morris County, most central AC systems last 12 to 15 years. Here is how to decide without guesswork.

## Start with these three factors

- **Age of the system:** 12 years or older is a warning sign
- **Cost of the repair:** if it is more than 40 to 50 percent of replacement cost, replacement is usually smarter
- **Comfort issues:** uneven cooling or constant repairs often means the system is undersized or worn out

## Signs replacement makes sense

- Major compressor or refrigerant leak repair
- Repeated breakdowns in the last two summers
- High electric bills even after maintenance
- Outdated refrigerant type

## When repair still makes sense

- System is under 10 years old
- The issue is a capacitor, contactor, or minor electrical part
- The system cools well once it is running
