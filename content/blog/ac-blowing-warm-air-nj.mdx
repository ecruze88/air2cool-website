When the AC is running but blowing warm air, it is usually not a thermostat issue. In North NJ, this often shows up when systems are stressed during hot, humid weeks.

## Common causes

- Low refrigerant from a leak
- Dirty condenser coils on the outdoor unit
- Tripped breaker for the outdoor unit
- Failed capacitor or contactor
- Frozen evaporator coil

## What you can check

- Make sure the thermostat is set to Cool and lower than room temp
- Check the outdoor unit for power and airflow
- Replace the air filter

If the outdoor unit is not running or the air stays warm, call a pro.

- Outdoor unit is silent or buzzing
- The system runs all day with no cooling
- You see ice on the indoor lines or coil
