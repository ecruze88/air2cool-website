Short cycling is when the AC starts, runs for a few minutes, then shuts off and repeats. It is common in North NJ during the first heat wave and it drives up electric bills fast.

## Why short cycling happens

- Oversized AC system
- Dirty air filter or clogged coil
- Low refrigerant
- Thermostat placement in direct sun
- Faulty capacitor or contactor

## Quick checks

- Replace the air filter
- Make sure the thermostat is not near a vent or window
- Confirm vents are open and unobstructed

If short cycling continues, stop running the system and call a pro.
