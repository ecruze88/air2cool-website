If the indoor fan is running but the outdoor unit is quiet, the system cannot cool. In North NJ, this is often a breaker, capacitor, or contactor issue.

## Safe checks you can do

- Confirm the thermostat is set to Cool and the temperature is lower than room temp
- Check the breaker for the outdoor unit
- Look for a shutoff switch near the outdoor unit and make sure it is on
- Clear debris so the unit can breathe

## Common causes

- Failed capacitor
- Bad contactor
- Tripped breaker or blown fuse
- Loose electrical connection

Do not open the electrical panel on the outdoor unit. High voltage is dangerous.

## When to call Air2Cool

- The breaker trips again after reset
- The unit hums but the fan does not spin
- You smell burning near the unit
