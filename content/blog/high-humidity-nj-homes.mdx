If your home feels sticky even though the AC is on, humidity is the issue. In North NJ, older homes and basements hold moisture, and standard AC alone is not always enough.

## Why humidity stays high

- Oversized AC that cools too fast and shuts off
- Dirty evaporator coil that cannot remove moisture
- Leaky ductwork pulling humid air from attics or basements
- No dedicated whole-home dehumidifier

## What you can do

- Replace the air filter and make sure airflow is strong
- Schedule a professional tune-up to clean coils
- Consider a whole-home dehumidifier for persistent issues

- Indoor humidity stays above 55 percent
- You see condensation on windows or ductwork
- Mold or musty odors appear during summer
